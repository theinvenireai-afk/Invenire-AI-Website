📚 Workshop Info

How Our Workshops Work

Two formats, one mission: making AI education accessible, exciting, and genuinely hands-on for every student we meet.

Workshop Formats

Choose Your Format

We offer two workshop formats — a multi-day deep-dive series and a single-day bootcamp. Both are free for nonprofit and publicly sponsored institutions. Detailed curricula coming soon!

📅

Multi-Day Workshop

45 min – 1 hr · 4–5 Days

Our full-depth AI learning series spanning multiple sessions. Students build knowledge day by day — from the basics of algorithms all the way to a final project they present to parents on the last day.

Each day covers a new theme: Algorithms, Machine Learning, Creative Collaboration, and two Project Days. Volunteers guide every station so every student gets real support.

🏫 For nonprofits & publicly sponsored institutions Sign Up for This Workshop →
⚡

Single-Day Bootcamp

3–4 Hours · One Day

The complete AI experience packed into one powerful session. Students go from zero to trained ML model in just a few hours — with games, unplugged activities, a mini-project, and a gallery walk showcase.

Perfect for community events, school days, or a student's very first encounter with artificial intelligence. No prior experience needed — just curiosity.
