About Us

Empowering the Next Generation of AI Thinkers

Invenire AI is a nonprofit on a mission to bring AI literacy to elementary and middle school students — exploring the basics, benefits, and ethics of artificial intelligence through hands-on learning.

We develop AI education programs centered around real tools, real creativity, and real impact. Every student leaves our workshops not just knowing what AI is, but understanding how to use, question, and build it.

Founded by passionate high school students who believe that AI education shouldn't wait for college — it starts now, and it starts with curiosity.

Our Mission

AI literacy is the new literacy.

🔍

Dive

We meet students where they are — no prior experience required. Every session starts with a game or activity that sparks immediate curiosity about how AI works.

💡

Discover

Students discover how AI actually works — not as magic, but as math, data, and decisions. Every concept is made tangible, accessible, and exciting.

🔧

Devise

Students don't just learn — they build. From trained ML models to Scratch projects, every student leaves with something they created and a skill they own.

"We don't just want our students to use the future — we want them to be the ones who build it. AI literacy is the new literacy."

— Invenire AI Founding Team
